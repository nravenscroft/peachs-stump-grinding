What we'll ask for

To quote accurately

  • Address / nearest cross streets
  • Number of stumps and approximate diameter
  • Photos of each stump and surrounding area
  • Gate/fence access (tight areas matter)
  • Any buried utilities/irrigation you know about
  • Whether you want chips left, spread, or removed

If you're unsure, send what you have — we'll guide you.

FAQs

Common questions

Do you offer free estimates? Yes — contact us for a free quote. Photos of the stump(s) and access help us estimate faster.
How deep do you grind? Typically below grade enough to restore the area; depth depends on the location and your plan for the space.
Will it damage my yard? We aim for a clean, careful approach. Access, terrain, and weather can affect results — we'll set expectations up front.
Do you remove the chips? Chips can be left for mulch or handled as an add-on. Tell us what you prefer.
